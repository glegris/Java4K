Mystery Mash
============

Description
-----------

A 4k mashup from the silver age of computer games based around Pong.

Instructions
------------

Play against the computer paddle, and avoid the incoming obstacles that can damage your paddle.

Controls:

mouse motion - Moving the mouse left and right moves the paddle. It also allows you to point to different parts of the screen.

mouse left click - Perform an action - release the ball from the paddle, turns all dropping Tetris pieces, and explodes the Missile Command missiles under the mouse.
