Jackal 4K
=========

Description
-----------

Battle through all 6 mission because your brothers-in-arms are hostages behind enemy lines and you're their only hope for freedom. But, the firepower you'll face to rescue them is awesome.

You'll need a pocket full of miracles and the ferocity of a wild JACKAL.

Your Jackal Squad is a four-man team composed of Colonel Decker, Lieut. Bob, Sgt. Quint and Corporal Grey.

And before you go, your country would just like to say, "Good luck, son. It will be worth it."


Instructions
------------

Use the arrow keys or the WASD keys to move. Use any other key to fire.
