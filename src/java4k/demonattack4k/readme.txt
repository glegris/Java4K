Demon Attack 4K
===============

Description
-----------

Marooned on the ice planet Krybor, you watch legions of eerie creatures scream overhead. They hover ominously. Attack and destroy them--or be destroyed! Armed with your Laser Cannon, you confront the ultimate challenge: Survive!

Survive 84 waves--and the demons will surrender! Your screen will go black.

Congratulations! You've joined a select few!

Instructions
------------

Use the arrow keys or the WASD keys to move. To the start game and to fire, press any other key with one exception: Press P to pause.
